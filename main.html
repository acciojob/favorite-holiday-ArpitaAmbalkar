Lonavla


Lonavala is a popular hill station in Maharashtra, situated in the Sahyadri range of the Western Ghats, renowned as a premier weekend getaway from Mumbai and Pune. It offers a serene escape with lush greenery, misty landscapes, and a variety of attractions including waterfalls, ancient caves, forts, and scenic viewpoints, making it ideal for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and families. The town is well-connected by road, rail, and air, with the nearest airport being Pune International Airport and the Lonavala Railway Station providing direct links to major cities.
